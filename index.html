WHO WE ARE, WHAT WE DO

Young Talent Charity Agency Sierra Leone is a voluntary and non profit organization, we have two sectors in our agency, first is to advocate for the needy ones and the other sector is to make people to show Case their talents.

MISSION STATEMENT

wish to work with global partnership organization and individual that has similar vision for the welfare and promotion of youth, children, women, girls and demote communities in needs to promote talent.

VISION STATEMENT

To see a smile on the less privilege faces to be love equally without no boundaries, race, age, status etc..

SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES

  • child protection: feeding,education, psychosocial camp, water and sanitation, promotion of talents.

  • ADVOCACY: media engagement, seminars or workshops, gender Base violence

  • GENDER EQUALITY: Agriculture or backyard gardening.
